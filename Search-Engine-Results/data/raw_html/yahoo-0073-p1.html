    The scientific name of the windpipe

    The scientific name of the windpipe is trachea. It is a vital part of the respiratory system, connecting the larynx to the bronchi of the lungs. The trachea is composed of cartilage rings that keep it open and is lined with mucous membranes that help trap particles and pathogens.
